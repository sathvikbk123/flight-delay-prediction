UNISYS Project

Prediction of arrival and departure delays of flights

Problem Statement

Airport operates on extremely tight margins and delays disrupt the operational throughput and result in bad passenger experience. The aim of this exercise is to identify the flights which are most likely to be delayed. In the given dataset, the flight schedule data is provided along with all related fields. You are free to augment this data set with any external data (eg. weather, socio-economic factors, etc) in your model.
